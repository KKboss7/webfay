Our Approach to Creating the Perfect Website For Your business

How we help you know what you need

Consultation

We begin by inquiring a lot of questions to better understand your company and your requirements for a website.

Planning

After the consultation, We develop a clear project plan that specifies particular tasks and outputs, as well as precise deadlines. We can clearly set and meet client expectations with the help of this plan.

Design

Now that we have the information that you will need, it's time to decide how your website will appear and feel.

Deployment

Before the website is officially launched, we open it for testing at this stage. Broken links, typos, missing design components, and device responsiveness are some of the things we test for. The website is then launched online.

Site Maintenance

Websites require ongoing care. An essential set of tasks that ensures a website is operating includes updating the backend, updating content, and fixing broken links. Building enduring relationships with our clients is important to us at Webfay. After your website launches, we guarantee continued maintenance and offer technical assistance.
